REQUIREMENTS TO STUDY IN CANADA

  • Obtain an acceptance letter from a recognized education provider before applying for a visa to study in Canada.
  • Research to find your suitability satisfying why study in Canada.
  • With visa application, you need to provide passport and photocopy, letter of acceptance from an institute, proof of English proficiency, academic documents, family information form, evidence of GIC, receipt of tuition fee payment, medical, student questionnaire, etc.

Living in CANADA

The requirements to study in Canada include purchasing a GIC, Guaranteed Investment Certificate. This is mandatory as it is towards accommodation of your first year living expenses. This amount is for the first year living expenses and is non-refundable.
